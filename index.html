Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the DoNote mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it.

Summary

DoNote is a to-do list app that stores all of your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information, and the App does not require an account or sign-in of any kind.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically, the App:

  • Does not require registration, login, or an account
  • Does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or physical address
  • Does not access your device's location, contacts, camera, or microphone
  • Does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services of any kind
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device only, using standard device storage (AsyncStorage):

  • To-do items you create (titles, categories, completion status, dates)
  • Categories you create or customize
  • Your app appearance preference (light, dark, or system theme)

This data never leaves your device. We do not have servers that receive, store, or process this information, and we cannot access it. If you uninstall the App, this data is permanently deleted from your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. No data is shared with any third party.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13, because it does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
